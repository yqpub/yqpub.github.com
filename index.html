About Me

Qian Yu is currently a PhD candidate at the Department of Systems Engineering and Engineering Management, The Chinese University of Hong Kong, under the supervision of Prof. Wai Lam. He has been working as a research intern in Alibaba DAMO Academy since Jan 2019, supervised by Dr. Lidong Bing. His current search interests include natural language processing and machine learning. He received BE and MS degree (supervised by Prof. Yuexian Hou) on Software Engineering from Tianjin University, China.
